Overview

Purpose

We rent property for vacationers that help prevent climate change. Our Rental is a great resource to see how to live organic, use sustainable energy, and enjoy the earth. With river trail biking, local gardening and groceries, and close to air-port convenience we hope that we can help you adopt environmental activism and still enjoy all the modern conveniences of the day at Bowers B & B. Bowers B & B offers a free organic breakfast grown in our gardens, the convenience of free electric transit (for up to 4 people) from and to the airport, and enjoyable nature adventures at next to no cost. With 14 mile friendly bike-rides that let you experience the nature of Sundance canyon, there is little else you will want from this relaxed stay. (Pet friendly stays are available at our Bed and Breakfast for an extra charge.)

Audience

Individuals, Couples and small families looking for lodging in the Utah-Provo area.

Typography

Heading Font: :

I want the name and logo to look like a mongrammed towel. I want the individuals that stay at Bowers B&B to feel like they are special because they chose to stay at our eco-friendly guest house. 5 star treatment for those that stay and 5 star treatment to the our planet as well.

Paragraph Font: :

I used the pairings feature in Google Fonts and really liked the way these two fonts complimented one-another.

Normal paragraph example:

Come visit the beautiful outdoor scenery of Provo Utah and help save our planet at the same time. Bowers B & B is within one mile of Utah Lake, where you can enjoy paddleboarding and beachside sun. Also within 5 blocks you can reach the Provo River trail where you may enjoy scooter rentals, fishing, and wasatch-front greenery. Wether you are walking, biking or running it is sure to be a pleasant stay.

Colored paragraph example

Trips vary from mild and great for families, to trips exclusively for physically fit and experienced vacationer. No matter what type of adventures you are seeking, Bowers B & B can make your vacation a 'guiltless' pleasure.
