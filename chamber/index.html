Elsinore Economic Expression

A commerce community for "Little Denmark" and her residing professionals.

History of Little Denmark:

"We recognize every small community progresses to a point in growth where the demand for more local resources being accessable is the priority. This can mean a closer grocery store, closer hardware retail, plumbers, or closer health care. Elsinore and her people are no exception and we want to create a Chamber community full of diverse, reliable, and unified businesses that meet our people's needs. This organization can be a great resourse to all that visit, live, and work here. Are you someone who could expand our community by providing a local trade or business to those around you? The Elsinore Chamber of Commerce team knows the difference you can make for it's citizens, your own family, and the town's future. The goal may seem far reaching, but every labor counts in "Little Denmark". What impact can you have if you are part of this?
