White Space & Clean Design: https://www.playbook.com/

Whitespace- is the area between the elements on a web page. Elements can mean typography, and icons. It is used to balance elements and create natural flow for the viewer. In the playbook.com page below you can see effective use of white space to allow the user to read the minimal content down to the "Get early Access" icon. The confetti helps to give a vibe of celebration be exhibited and also helps in the flow of reading the content.

Rule of Thirds: https://foundation.gucci.com/

The Rule of Thirds- means dividing an image or layout into thirds both vertically and horizontally, creating a grid with nine equal-sized sections.The Gucci page below is a perfect example of how the arms cross in the middle and the other 8 sections surround it.

Repetition: https://www.ae.com/us/en

Repetition- means repeating a single element many times in a design. In this example you see each image repeats the same kind of landscape.
